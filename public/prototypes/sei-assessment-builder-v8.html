Interview · March 15, 2026 · 47 min
Consultant
Dana, can you walk me through the primary business challenge you're hoping AI could address in the next 90 days?
Dana Reyes — COO
Loan underwriting is taking us 18 days on average. Our competitors are at 7. We lose deals in that window. If AI can close even half that gap, it changes our competitive position meaningfully.
Consultant
What's the primary constraint — data availability, process complexity, or something else?
Dana Reyes — COO
Honestly it's both. The data lives in three different systems and the business unit thinks they own it, but IT controls access. We've tried to run projects before and they've stalled at exactly that point.
Consultant
Has the compliance function been involved in any AI planning so far?
Dana Reyes — COO
Not formally. Sarah Kim is aware but there's no governance framework in place. That's a gap I know we need to close.
